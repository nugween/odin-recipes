Chocolate Chip Cookie

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour - 2 1/4 cups to be exact.
  • Baking soda - 1 teaspoon
  • Salt - 1 teaspoon
  • Butter (softened) - 1 cup (or 2 sticks)
  • Granulated sugar - 3/4 cups
  • Packed brown sugar - 3/4 cup
  • Vanilla extract - 1 teaspoon
  • Eggs - 2
  • Chocolate chips - Very important! 2 cups. More if you REALLY like chocolate.

How to make it

  1. Preheat oven - 375F
  2. Combine - Combine flour, baking soda and salt in a small bowl.
  3. Beat Beat butter , granulated sugar, brown sugar and vanilla extract in large mixer bowl until creamy.
  4. Add eggs - Add one at a time, beating well after each one.
  5. Combine - Gradually add in flour mixture.
  6. Best part - Add in chocolate chips.
  7. Roll into balls - Roll freshly made cookie dough into palm-sized balls and put on baking sheet.
  8. Bake - Bake for 9 to 11 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Rest - Let cool for 2 minutes and enjoy.
