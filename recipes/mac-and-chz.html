Mac and Cheese

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni - The go to pasta for mac and cheese. Can swap for another bite sized pasta.
  • Butter - Salted or unsalted, can adjust salt amount later.
  • Flour - Use to make roux.
  • Salt - Always.
  • Ground black pepper - Add a little excitement (can replace with a spice like cajun)
  • Milk - Help make it creamier.
  • Half and half - Balances the flavor.
  • Shredded cheddar cheese - 1 cup. The higher quality aged-cheddar, the better!

Make it!

  1. Preheat the oven - Preheat to 325 degrees and lightly grease a square baking dish.
  2. Cook the macaroni - Slightly under cook your noodles (about a minute under al-dente). Drain and set aside.
  3. Make the roux - Melt the butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Blend in the flour, salt, and pepper. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add millk and cheese - Stir in milk and half and half, slowly, stirring constantly. Remove from heat. Add 1 cup shredded cheese to the sauce and stire just untilmelted. Add the cooked macaroni noodles and toss to coat them in the sauce.
  5. Pour into baking dish - Pour half or the pasta mixture into the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle 1/2 cup cheese over the top. Pour the remaining pasta over it and sprinkle with remaining cheese.
